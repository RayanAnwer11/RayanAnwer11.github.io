Current Projects

Current Tester

A leakage current tester is a device used to measure the small amounts of current that flow through insulation or unintended paths in electrical equipment. It helps identify potential faults or hazards by evaluating the insulation integrity.

EKG Tester

An EKG tester device, also called an EKG machine or ECG machine, is used to measure and record the electrical activity of the heart. It helps diagnose heart conditions by displaying or recording the heart's electrical signals.

An Android-controlled wheelchair is a wheelchair that can be operated using an Android device, such as a smartphone or tablet. It enables individuals to control the wheelchair's movements and functions through a dedicated Android application.
